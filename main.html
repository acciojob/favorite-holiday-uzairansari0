Tokyo, Japan


Tokyo is a dazzling metropolis where ultra-modern skyscrapers and neon-lit streets coexist with historic temples and serene gardens. As the world's most populous city, it offers an endless array of shopping, entertainment, and world-class dining experiences.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The historic Senso-ji Temple, Tokyo's oldest Buddhist site in Asakusa.
  2. Watching the organized chaos of the Shibuya Scramble Crossing.
  3. A visit to the Meiji Jingu Shrine, a peaceful oasis in the heart of the city.
  4. The panoramic city views from the Tokyo Skytree, the world's tallest tower.
  5. Exploring the immersive digital art at teamLab Planets.

Must-Try Foods

  • Authentic Edomae Sushi, originally the "fast food" of old Tokyo.
  • A steaming bowl of Tonkotsu Ramen with a rich, creamy broth.
  • Savory Yakitori (grilled chicken skewers) found in hidden alleyways.
  • Light and crispy Tempura served with a delicate dipping sauce.
  • Sweet Taiyaki, a fish-shaped pastry filled with red bean paste.
